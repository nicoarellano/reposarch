About

My name is Keegan Metheringham. I was born on the banks of the Red Deer River some time in the Spring of 1773. I spent my early years working as a fur trader for the North West Company trapping mostly beavers and marmot. But after suffering a bear attack in the Rocky Foothills, I was forced to give up trapping and trading. I next headed to the Pacific Coast with Alexander McKenzie. After traversing the Rockies we reached the Bella Coola river. I went native and spent some time with with several different groups of Haida peoples. They showed me their ways and how abundant the natural resources were. They also showed me a deep respect for nature, which I had never seen before. After a few years of this, I ended up in a small coal town on the Eastern Coast of the largest Island. I've been there ever since.
